ABOUT US

Hibbert Luxury Essentials (HLE) is a subleasing business focused on providing others who are traveling the globe with a place to lay their heads while on vacation. But, not just any place to indulge in a night of sleep... here at HLE we combine the feel of a luxury stay with worldclass services.

To us, it is not just about having somewhere to stay for the time being while on vacation. Rather, it has to do with feeling comfortable in the state, city, and immediate area you are in. It is about the quality and significance of your stay that motive us to provide our best work.

This business was built by individuals who are passionate in the field of helping others. Kindness, attentivness, and cleanliness are some of the words that captivate who we are, what we provide, and how we go about doing so!
